Sammy B

Hi, my name is Sam Bernard aka SammyB. I’ve been tattooing for 10 years, tattooing professionally for about 5 years now but I’ve been an artist my whole life. Starting out in Las Vegas NV, moved to southern Utah, helped build Freddys Ink in Hurricane Utah and worked there for about 3-4 years before moving over to the Garrison Tattoo Company. I specialize in line work, geometric, neo-traditional, illustrative and realism. Big time anime fan like Dragon ball z, Naruto and Hunter x Hunter. Father of 3 kiddos
